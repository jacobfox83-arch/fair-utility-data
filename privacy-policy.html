FAIR Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

FAIR ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

FAIR does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. We collect limited anonymous usage data to improve the app experience:

  • Usage analytics - which countries, regions and utility tabs you view through Google Analytics 4. This data is anonymous and cannot identify you personally.
  • Crash reports - anonymous error reports to help us fix bugs through Sentry.
  • Advertising data - Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads.

Advertising

FAIR uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device advertising ID to show personalised or non-personalised ads depending on your choices and regional consent requirements.

You can change advertising preferences in your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your precise location
  • Payment or financial information
  • Information from children under 13

Third-Party Services

FAIR uses Google Analytics 4, Sentry, and Google AdMob. Each service has its own privacy policy and processing terms.

Children's Privacy

FAIR is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will change when we do.
